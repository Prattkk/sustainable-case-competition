## 📖 Overview
This repository contains the strategic framework and technical prototype developed for a high-stakes sustainability case competition. Our team proposed an AI-driven ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between corporate sustainability goals and academic research, specifically aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

## ⚖️ Business Problem
Organizations face significant hurdles in translating high-level sustainability targets into operational reality:
* **Data Silos:** Impact data is often fragmented across departments and research papers.
* **SDG Alignment:** Difficulty in mapping diverse activities to specific global targets.
* **Accessibility:** Stakeholders lack a "natural language" interface to query complex sustainability datasets.

## 💡 Solution Summary
Our solution, the **Sustainability Impact Engine**, features a two-tiered architecture:
1.  **Impact Command Center:** A high-fidelity dashboard for real-time KPI tracking.
2.  **Research Navigator:** An AI agent that leverages NLP to identify research synergies and provide decision support for SDG-aligned investments.
